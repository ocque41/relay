# Trademark Policy

The source code in this repository is open source. The Cumulus and Relay names,
logos, domains, and brand assets may be kept in unmodified generated projects
and forks, but they do not grant control of the official Cumulus Cloud service,
domains, npm package names, or social accounts.

You may:

- refer to this project as Cumulus Relay,
- say that your project is based on or compatible with Cumulus Relay,
- use the names in truthful technical documentation,
- keep the Relay/Cumulus branding and visual identity emitted by
  `create-cumulus`,
- keep unmodified references that identify upstream source code.

You may not, without permission:

- present your fork as the official Cumulus Relay service,
- claim that your hosted fork is operated, endorsed, or supported by Cumulus
  unless we have agreed to that in writing,
- register confusingly similar domains, package names, or social accounts,
- remove notices in a way that hides upstream provenance.

If you host a public fork, make the operator clear in your README, footer, and
legal pages.
